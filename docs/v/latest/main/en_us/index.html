      Conjure Tools

      During one of my recent expeditions, I seem to have accidentally discovered a way to manipulate matter itself using media. Specifically the creation of it, or rather the illusion of reforming air particles around me. However, doing so is incredibly difficult, and so I have devised a simple method to make it easier.


      By focusing on a mere concept, such as a tool of some sorts, the media seems to do the rest. I've appropriately named these concepts 'wishes'.

      Each wish seems to cost the same amount of media, about a tenth of an amethyst dust per point of durability, plus an extra 2.
